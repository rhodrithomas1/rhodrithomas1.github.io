About This Site

Welcome to my little corner of the cosmos! I’m Rhodri Thomas—a stargazer, amateur astrophotographer, and software tinkerer. This site is where I share my latest deep-sky images, showcase the tools I’ve built to plan and process them, and experiment with dark-mode web design inspired by the night sky.

Under the hood it’s a simple static site hosted on GitHub Pages, styled with a custom dark theme and built by hand in HTML, CSS, and a bit of JavaScript. Every photo in the “Astro Photos” gallery is captured through my backyard setup, while the “Projects” page is where I dive into code—like my DeepSkyScout app for horizon-aware imaging planning.

Whether you’re here to browse nebulae or peek at my source code, thanks for stopping by. Feel free to explore, star my projects on GitHub, or drop me a note if you want to chat about telescopes, Python, or web design under the stars!
